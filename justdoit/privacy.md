# Privacy Policy for JustDoit

**Last updated: November 22, 2025**

We respect your privacy. "JustDoit" is designed to be private by default.

## Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on our servers.
- **Tasks Data**: All your tasks are stored locally on your device and synced securely via your personal iCloud account (CloudKit). We cannot access this data.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., crash reports, logs) to improve the app, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues: [\[GitHub Issues\]](https://github.com/sjpsega/my-apps/issues)
